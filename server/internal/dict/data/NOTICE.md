# Third-party data attribution

`cedict.json.gz` is derived from **CC-CEDICT**, a community Chinese-English
dictionary, filtered to HSK 3.0 vocabulary and single characters and reformatted
(numbered pinyin converted to tone marks).

- Source: https://www.mdbg.net/chinese/dictionary?page=cc-cedict
